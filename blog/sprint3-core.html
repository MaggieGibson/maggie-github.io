How is it different to IQ?

EQ is defined as an individuals ability to identify, evaluate, control and express emotions, whereas IQ is a score calculated from multiple tests designed to assess an individuals intelligence



Why is it important?

Emotional intelligence allows you to manage and understand your emotions as well as others around you, leading you to be more empathetic and self-motivating. Knowing when to be sympathetic, encouraging, professional in other words knowing how to respond and identify emotions is important because it gives you the ability to make correct judgment and or response to various scenarios allowing you to succeed and grow on a more personal level.
